Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Cohen Adair built Activity Log as a commercial app. This service is provided by Cohen Adair and is intended for use as is.

This page informs visitors of policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for anyone who uses the service.

By using the service, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. Personal information is used only for providing and improving the service, and is not shared with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

All activity data you enter is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server. When you voluntarily submit feedback via the in-app feedback form, certain information is transmitted as described in the "Feedback Form" section below.

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services (Android only)
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Analytics
  • RevenueCat
  • SendGrid (feedback form delivery)

Feedback Form

When you submit feedback through the app, the following information is sent to SendGrid, a third-party email delivery service, in order to deliver your message to the support team:

  • Your name and email address (only if you choose to provide them)
  • Device model, operating system version, and device identifier
  • App version
  • A RevenueCat customer ID (an anonymous identifier used to look up your subscription status)

This data is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your feedback and is deleted thereafter.

Log Data

In the event of an error, data and information may be collected via third-party products (Log Data). This may include your device's IP address, device name, operating system version, app configuration, time and date of use, and other statistics. Firebase Crashlytics also receives your device locale and a RevenueCat customer ID as a pseudonymous user identifier to help correlate crash reports.

Cookies

This service does not use cookies explicitly. However, third-party code and libraries used by the service may use cookies to collect information and improve their services.

Service Providers

Third-party companies and individuals may be employed to facilitate the service, provide the service on the creator's behalf, or assist in analyzing how the service is used. These parties have access to personal information solely to perform tasks assigned on the creator's behalf, and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Security

Your trust in providing personal information is valued. Commercially acceptable means are used to protect it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Links to Other Sites

This service may contain links to other sites. Clicking a third-party link directs you to that site. These external sites are not operated by this service, and it is recommended to review their privacy policies. The service has no control over and assumes no responsibility for their content, privacy policies, or practices.

Children's Privacy

This service does not address anyone under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If such information is discovered, it is immediately deleted.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data held about you (for example, information submitted via the feedback form). To exercise any of these rights, please contact: support@activitylog.ca

Changes to This Privacy Policy
